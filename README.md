Supporting Information includes Pearson correlations between WHO and Worldometer reported cases; Pearson correlations between Google Trends data and Worldometer reported cases; daily reported COVID-19 cases by WHO and Worldometer in Africa, Americas, Asia and Oceania, and Europe; predicted and WHO cases in Africa, Americas, Asia and Oceania, and Europe; predicted and Worldometer cases in Africa, Americas, Asia and Oceania, and Europe.
